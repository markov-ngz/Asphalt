## Architecture Overview

Asphalt’s architecture is divided into four layers:

- [**Domain :**](domain.md)
  Business terminology and standards (e.g., GTFS, vehicle telemetry).

- [**Data :**](data.md)
  Data model, schemas, and evolution strategies.


- [**Applicative**](applicative.md)
  Real-time processing (Flink, Kafka) and event-driven workflows.

- [**Technical :**](technical.md)
  Deployment (Kubernetes), monitoring, and observability.
